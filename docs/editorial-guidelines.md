# MENSVIEW SENSE Editorial Guidelines

## Product Page Source of Truth

- For Rakuten affiliate product LPs, `PRODUCT_PAGE_RULES.md` is the default source of truth.
- New product pages should follow its section order, CTA roles, typography rules, NG vocabulary check, and "楽天で要確認" price/stock wording.
- `scripts/build_site.py` owns the generated HTML structure, so update that generator before editing generated `products/*.html` by hand.

## Persona

- Main audience: late-20s to early-30s working women.
- Editorial voice: a same-generation male editor, expressed mainly through the site about section.
- Product copy should avoid first person. Do not use "私" or "自分" as the writer's subject.

## Placement

- Keep the male point of view out of product H1s, captions, and benefit copy.
- The site-name rationale belongs in the about section only.
- Product pages should frame appeal as third-party impressions: "誰が来ても", "来客の視線", "ふと褒められる", "感じがいい".

## Do Not Use

- 男ウケ
- モテ
- 彼ウケ
- 惚れ直す
- 男心をくすぐる
- 女子力
- 愛され
- 〇〇な女になる
- repeated use of 映え or ご褒美

## Preferred Vocabulary

- 感じがいい
- 整っている
- 清潔感
- 余白
- 抜け感
- 誰が来ても
- 来客の視線
- ふと褒められる
- 人を呼びたくなる
- 長く使える
- 過不足ない

## Density Rule

Use the implied third-party appeal at most once per page. More than that makes the tone too direct.
